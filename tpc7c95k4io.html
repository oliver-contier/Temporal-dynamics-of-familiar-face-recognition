Progression over runs:
To investigate the time course / progression of the observed habituation effects, we extracted the mean parameter estimates within the thresholded clusters found in the reversed slope of the contrast Familiar > Unfamiliar from the first level analysis. Figure \ref{249356} shows that the respective progression of these
contrast Familiar > Unfamiliar is significantly greater than zero during the first three runs and decreases during the following runs, being significantly below zero in runs 8 and 10.
