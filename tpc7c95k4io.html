Progression over runs:
To further explore the time course of the responses to unfamiliar faces, familiar faces as well as the contrast Familiar > Unfamiliar, we extracted the mean parameter estimates within the thresholded clusters found in the reversed slope of the contrast Familiar > Unfamiliar (see Figure \ref{563705} B) from the first level analysis. Figure \ref{249356} shows the mean parameter estimates within these regions per run for all subjects.
The contrast Familiar > Unfamiliar (Figure \ref{249356}, right panel) generally decreases during the course of the experiment, with pronounced positive values above zero during the first three runs and negative values during runs eight and ten.
